    What You'll Need

    Required Documents

    Gather these documents before starting your application to speed up the process.

    • Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
    • Social Security Number (SSN) or Individual Taxpayer ID (ITIN)
    • Proof of residency (utility bill, lease, or bank statement)
    • Recent pay stubs or proof of income (last 30 days)
    • Most recent federal tax return (if available)
    • Birth certificates for any children in your household
    • Proof of citizenship or immigration status
    • Bank account information (for direct deposit of benefits)

    Need help gathering documents?

    Visit any local government office for free assistance. Bring what you have ‚Äî our staff can help with the rest.
