Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Need more help? Contact our team.

You can renew your driver's license up to 12 months before the expiration date. Your new license will retain the same expiration month but advance 5 years from your current expiration date. Renewing early does not cause you to lose any validity time.

To obtain a REAL ID-compliant card, you must present in person: (1) proof of identity with full legal name, (2) proof of SSN, (3) two proofs of state residency. REAL ID cannot be applied for or renewed online ‚Äî an in-person DMV visit is required. The REAL ID upgrade fee is $5 above the standard license fee.

It depends on your business type. Most business licenses allow provisional operation upon application submission and payment of fees while the review is in progress. However, food service, healthcare, and professional licenses require full approval before operations begin. Check your specific license type for interim operating rules.

If your license is lost or stolen, you can request a duplicate online through your MyGov account for a $15 replacement fee. Alternatively, visit any DMV office with proof of identity. Your replacement card will carry the same expiration date as your original license. Report a stolen license to local police if your identity may have been compromised.
